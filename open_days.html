    • VISITING FAB LAB BARCELONA

    Are you curious to see what happens inside FabLab Barcelona?

    INDIVIDUALS

    We open our doors to receive individual visitors for approx. half an hour free tour once a month. If you would like to visit us, please register for the next available visit here. Unfortunately we are not able to host or let in any individuals or groups that are not previously booked and confirmed.

    GROUPS

    If you are a group of minimum 10 people, you can book the more in-depth 2 hour “FabLab Experience”. Please contact matias@fablabbcn.org to book a group tour.

    Group visit pricing (2hrs)

    Non-profits and schools: 20€ per person

    Others: 40€ per person

    The FabLab Experience includes:

    • Introduction to Digital Fabrication and Fab Labs ecosystem
    • Tour through the Fab Lab Barcelona and demo of the Digital Fabrication technologies
    • Introduction to Fab Lab Barcelona and some projects
    • Fab City Global Initiative - how can cities become self sufficient by 2054?
    • Tour through the @22 | Poblenou Urban District (if time permits)
